About us Highlights

Since 1975, our family has proudly welcomed guests like they are part of our own home. Thank you for supporting our small business!

Ethiopian food is rich flavors, unique spices and shared dining experience. Meals are traditionally served on a large sourdough flatbread called injera, which is used to scoop up delicious stews and vegetables.

Popular dishes include spicy lentils (misir wot), slow-cooked beef stews (key wot), and sautéed vegetables. Ethiopian cuisine often combines bold spices like berbere and fresh herbs to create warm, flavorful meals.Food is more than just a meal in Ethiopia it is a time for family, community, and connection.
